What is Fantasy Football?

Fantasy football is a game in which participants (fantasy managers) pick an imaginary team of real life footballers and score points based on those players' actual statistical performance. Your team will score points based on: goals scored, clean sheets, appearances, penalty saves, assists, yellow cards, red cards etc.

Fantasy football will give you an opportunity to test your football knowledge, challenge your friends and prove your worth as the region's greatest fantasy football manager. Our version is based on the English Premier League and will be played via text message and online. The objective of the game (for the participants) is to score the highest total points whilst being within budget allocation at the end of the season. Several cash prizes will be given away to top managers throughout the season.


How to play

Our experts have given each player a fantasy value based on their popularity and on how we think they will perform during the season.

It is upon you to make the best selection that will enable you win tremedous amounts of money


Player Selection

To start managing your team to glory, select an 11 man team with a budget of $50M. Your team will score points during the season based on how well your selected players perform in real life.You will have several opportunities throughout the season to change your team and win various cash prizes along the way.

You need to choose 11 players that have a combined value of $50M or less. The player list will show the players allocated positions e.g. GK, DEF, MID, STR.

Scoring system

Rule Points
Goal Scored 5 for each player who scores
Player scoring 3 or more goals 5 bonus points for the player
Clean Sheet (keeper/defender) 5 for each GK/Def
Clean Sheet (midfielder) 2 for each mid fielder
Conceded more than one goal (keeper/defender) -1 per goal for each GK/Def
Penalty save (keeper) 5 for the GK
Yellow Card -1 for player
Red Card -3 in total for player (overrules yellow card)
Starting a game 2 for all players (first 11)
Substitute 1 for each sub
Penalty Miss -2 points for the penalty taker

Making the right choice

Your selection must fit into either 442 (4 defenders, 4 midfielders and 2 strikers) or 433 (4 defenders, 3 midfielders and 3 strikers) and your team must always have a goalkeeper. You will definitely have a lot to consider when making your selection. Will you opt for the league’s new acquisitions such as: Anthony Martial, Kevin de Bruyne and Dmitri Payet OR will you go for the tried and tested players from seasons gone by like: Aguero, Rooney and Hazard. Keep a keen eye on all the football schedules, there are always bargains in the transfer market. Want to play? Start Here!

Captains

You must select a team captain. Your team captain will earn DOUBLE points from each round of games. These captains can be changed every week while the transfer window is open

Online: To select a captain using the online version, just click on the star on the player icon after selecting your 11 man team.

SMS: To select a captain using the SMS version, just send the word "FF CAPTAIN [player code]" to 8338 e.g. "FF Captain 121"

Transfers

If you are not happy with your team selection the first time round, you can always ditch the flops and replace them with fresh legs. Every week, we will have transfer windows where you will be able to add to your team. The changes of course, will still have to be within your $50M budget.

The transfer windows will be closed 30 minutes before kickoff
